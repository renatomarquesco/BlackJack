• The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.
  • Face cards(KQJ) are worth 10. Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever makes a better hand.
  • Each player starts with two cards, one of the dealer's cards is hidden until the end.
  • To 'Hit' is to ask for another card. To 'Stand' is to hold your total and end your turn.
  • If you go over 21 you bust, and the dealer wins regardless of the dealer's hand
  • If you are dealt 21 from the start (Ace & 10), you got a blackjack and win the hand.
  • Dealer will hit until his/her cards total 17 or higher.
  • Dealer can't hit if his/her hand is worth more than 17.
